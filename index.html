About Pat's Savory Salmon Cookies

They're cookies made with salmon and shaped like salmon, too! We only use the finest organic, cage free, wild caught salmon. The fish is ground up so fine that you can't even taste it, but you'll get all the benefits! These cookies are the ideal match for a steaming cup of coffee on a gray Seattle morning... Or gray Seattle afternoon... Or gray Seattle evening. Tourists will love them, and you locals will begrudgingly fall in love with them, too.
